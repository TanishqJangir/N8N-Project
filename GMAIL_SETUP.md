
## Step 1: Enable 2-Step Verification (2FA)
Before you can create an App Password, your Google Account must have 2-Step Verification enabled.
1. Go to your [Google Account Dashboard](https://myaccount.google.com/).
2. On the left navigation panel, click on **Security**.
3. Under "How you sign in to Google," look for **2-Step Verification**.
4. If it's turned off, click it and follow the on-screen prompts to enable it. (You will need a phone number).

## Step 2: Create an App Password
1. Once 2-Step Verification is on, go directly to the [App Passwords page](https://myaccount.google.com/apppasswords).
   *(If the link doesn't work, go to **Security** \> search for "App passwords" in the search bar at the top).*
2. You will be prompted to sign in again.
3. Under "App name," type something descriptive like **"Nodebase Workflow"** or **"Localhost App"**.
4. Click **Create**.
5. A modal will pop up displaying a 16-character password (e.g., `abcd efgh ijkl mnop`). 
6. **Copy this 16-character password.** This is your new `GMAIL_APP_PASSWORD`.


Your Gmail node should now work perfectly!
